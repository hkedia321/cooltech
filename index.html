ABOUT COOL TECH


Cool Tech was established in 1980. Since then Cool Tech has become one of the largest supplier of Air conditioning and refrigerating spare parts in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, including a wide sales network.

Cool Tech top priority is customer satisfaction. We achieve that first and foremost through our unbeatable service. We also ensure we provide as many options as possible to make that service convenient and stress-free.

We remain committed to provide environmentally responsible and technically superior products / components / spare parts from the industry's most leading brands, both from domestic and international segments.
